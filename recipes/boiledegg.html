Boiled Egg

    Ingredients
  • Egg
  • Water
    Directions
  1. Put a single layer of eggs into a pot not much bigger than the total number of eggs you wish to prepare.
  2. Fill room temperature water into the pot just over the tops of the eggs.
  3. Put the pot onto the stove and set the heat on high.
  4. Set a timer
    • Soft Gooey - 11 Minutes
    • Soft Cooked - 13 Minutes
    • Hard Boiled - 15 Minutes
  5. Dump the hot water, put the eggs into a bowl of cold ice water to rest for ten minutes.
  6. Peel and Enjoy!
